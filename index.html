What if you could go back in time?

About Time is a heartwarming romantic drama that explores love, family, and time travel. When Tim discovers he can travel through time, he uses his gift to create the perfect life—but soon realizes that the beauty of life is in it's imperfections.

"The truth is, I now don't travel back at all, not even for the day. I just try to live every day as if I've deliberately come back to this one day to enjoy it, as if it was the full final day of my extraordinary, ordinary life."

Meet the Characters

Tim Lake(Domhnall Gleeson)

"I quite liked the idea that you could do something where people like each other, and then there was the time travel and then they liked each other again."

Mary(Rachel McAdams)

"It's the messiness of life that ultimately leads you to the most interesting things."

Tim's Dad(Bill Nighy)

It's a film about how you should relish every normal day and not wait for special occasions. It's a beautiful idea and a beautiful script."

Richard Curtis(Director)

"I came up with a very simple idea, which is: What would you do if you could live every day twice? And that turned into a film which was a bit about love, but actually, in the end, much more about trying to make the most of every ordinary day."
